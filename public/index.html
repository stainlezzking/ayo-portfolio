About me

Alex Fernando Johnson- hailed from bogota columbia is An American business Mogul and economist Having doctorate degrees in both London school of business and the New York business school Having honorary degrees in cooperative economics and stock brokerage And also business administration and management in San Francisco state university. After earning a degree in Finance, he quickly learned the ins and outs of the investment business as a stock and forex broker, financial advisor, crypto mining and professional money manager.

He even served as a Vice President for a major investment bank and held series 7, series 66 and Series 3 securities licenses. Alex has a simple investment philosophy follow - Follow The Money. He has dedicated years to studying volume patterns that reveal pending moves in under-the-radar stocks. Alex is also the leading expert on insider trading. His proprietary system follows the buying and selling of corporate insider to identify high-probability investment opportunities. He has been featured on Fox Business, CNBC, Bloomberg, CNBC Awaaz, Zee Business and several other major financial networks. He currently handles a trading portfolio for alot of investment and Fintech Companies globally

OUR CLIENTS

Our clients’ wealth is treated with the same care as our own. We take considerable responsibility as stewards of that wealth, so our clients can enjoy financial independence and increased flexibility. Our clients typically have more than one million of investable assets, allowing us to serve them with the depth, focus, and attention they deserve.

We are blessed with a practice built upon trust and referrals: Our clients regularly ask us to take care of their family and friends; further, our clients’ professional advisors end up entrusting their other client This ongoing affirmation of our work is integral to our business and inspires us to always do our best for our clients. We recognize that our role goes beyond the dollar to impact the broader lives of our clients and their communities.

Many of our clients identify with the following

Professionals—Professionals across various industries have accumulated wealth over time. We help them build on this foundation of savings with careful retirement planning and tax optimization

Executives—Busy executives have incredible demands and expectations placed upon them. We ensure their finances are taken care of through investments, diversification, estate-planning and tax-mitigation strategies

Retirees—Many of our clients are able to retire comfortably because we help them live off revenue generated from their portfolios, so they can pursue leisure activities, philanthropic efforts, and time with loved ones.

Divorcees— Divorcees are faced with settlement challenges and emotional upheaval, all while transitioning to independently managing their finances. We empower them with new knowledge and divorce-specific financial analysis so they are better prepared to evaluate settlement proposals in coordination with their legal and tax counsel

Business Owners—Difficult work goes into preparing for business succession. We help develop the most suitable strategy to achieve succession goals, whether through Employee Stock Ownership Plans, retirement plans, training up key employees, or facilitating a sale

Sudden Wealth—Inheriting money or experiencing a liquidity event brings tremendous opportunity and responsibility. We coach our clients on disciplined and tax efficient diversification strategies helping them to manage their wealth and reduce their risk
